Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: How do I create an account?
A: Open the Fortunia app and tap "Sign Up" on the authentication screen. Enter your email address and create a secure password. You'll receive a verification email to complete the registration.
Q: How do I log in if I forgot my password?
A: On the login screen, tap "Forgot Password?" and enter your email address. You'll receive a password reset link via email. Follow the instructions in the email to create a new password.
Q: Why can't I upload a photo?
A: Make sure you've granted camera permissions to Fortunia. Go to iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Camera > Fortunia, and ensure it's enabled. Also, ensure you have enough storage space on your device.
Q: My reading is taking too long to process. What should I do?
A: AI processing typically takes 5-15 seconds. If it's taking longer:
  • Check your internet connection
  • Close and reopen the app
  • Try again later if our servers are experiencing high traffic
Premium users get priority processing and should see faster results.
Q: How do the free readings work?
A: Free users get 3 readings per day at no cost. After you've used your 3 free readings, you can either wait until the next day (quota resets at midnight UTC) or upgrade to Premium for unlimited readings.
Q: How do I upgrade to Premium?
A: When you've used your daily free readings, tap "Upgrade to Premium" on the paywall screen. You can choose between Monthly ($9/month) or Yearly ($55/year) subscriptions. Payment is processed securely through Apple.
Q: Can I cancel my Premium subscription?
A: Yes, you can cancel anytime through your Apple ID account settings. Go to Settings > [Your Name] > Subscriptions > Fortunia > Cancel Subscription. You'll continue to have access until the end of your current billing period.
Q: How do I share my fortune readings?
A: After viewing your reading, tap the share button (the icon with an upward arrow). You can share via Messages, Mail, Social Media, or save to your photo library. Share cards are optimized for Instagram stories.
Q: Are the fortune readings real predictions?
A: No, the readings are AI-generated for entertainment purposes only. They are not based on real fortune-telling or actual predictions about your future. Please see our Terms of Service and Privacy Policy for more information.
Q: Why do I need to grant camera access?
A: Fortunia uses your device's camera to capture photos for face, palm, and coffee readings. These photos are uploaded to our secure servers and processed by AI to generate your personalized readings. You can revoke camera access in iOS Settings > Privacy & Security > Camera at any time.
Q: What if I don't want to create an account?
A: You can use Fortunia as a guest user with a device identifier. However, creating an account allows you to:
  • Save your reading history
  • Access readings on multiple devices
  • Manage subscription purchases
  • Request data deletion if needed

Technical Troubleshooting

App Crashes or Freezes

  • Force close and restart: Swipe up from the bottom (on iPhone X or later) or double-press the home button (on older iPhones), swipe up on Fortunia to close it, then reopen.
  • Update the app: Go to the App Store and check for Fortunia updates.
  • Restart your device: Hold the power button and slide to power off, then turn your device back on.
  • Reinstall the app: Delete and reinstall Fortunia from the App Store (your account data is stored in the cloud, so you won't lose anything).

Image Upload Failures

  • Check internet connection: Ensure you're connected to Wi-Fi or cellular data.
  • Check file size: Extremely large images may fail to upload. Try taking a new photo or compressing it.
  • Grant permissions: Ensure Fortunia has permission to access photos and camera in iOS Settings > Privacy & Security.
  • Try a different image: The current photo may be corrupted.

Premium Features Not Unlocking

  • Restore purchases: In the app, go to Settings > Restore Purchases.
  • Check Apple ID: Ensure you're signed into the same Apple ID that made the purchase.
  • Wait a few minutes: Subscription activation can take up to 5 minutes after purchase.
  • Contact support: If issues persist, email us with your Apple receipt.
