Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2025

Welcome to Fortunia ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal information when you use our mobile application Fortunia (the "App").

By using Fortunia, you consent to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with our practices, please do not use the App.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information You Provide

  • Account Information: When you create an account, we collect your email address and password (which is encrypted and never stored in plain text).
  • Birth Information: If you choose to provide it for personalized readings, we collect your birth date, birth time, birth city, and birth country. This information is used solely to personalize your AI-generated fortune readings.
  • Language Preferences: We store your preferred language setting for the app interface.

1.2 Images You Capture

  • Camera Access: With your permission, we access your device's camera to capture photos for fortune readings (face reading, palm reading, and coffee ground readings).
  • Image Storage: These images are uploaded to and stored securely in our Supabase Storage cloud servers. Images are associated with your user account and used to generate AI-powered fortune readings.
  • Image Processing: Your images are analyzed by Google's Gemini AI service to generate personalized fortune readings. We do not use your images for any other purpose.
  • Image Retention: Images are retained on our servers to maintain your reading history. You can request deletion at any time.

1.3 Automatically Collected Information

  • Device Information: We collect your iOS device model, operating system version, and app version for debugging and improving the app experience.
  • Usage Analytics: We track app usage patterns, including which features you use, how often you use them, and performance metrics. This helps us improve the app.
  • Crash Reports: If the app crashes, we automatically collect error logs to help us fix bugs. This includes technical information about your device and app state at the time of the crash.
  • Network Information: We collect basic network information to ensure reliable service delivery.

1.4 Third-Party Service Data

  • Supabase: Your account data, reading history, and uploaded images are stored in Supabase's secure cloud infrastructure.
  • Firebase: We use Firebase Analytics and Firebase Crashlytics for app analytics and crash reporting. These services collect device identifiers and usage data.
  • Google Gemini AI: Your images and reading requests are processed by Google's Gemini AI API to generate fortune readings.
  • Apple StoreKit: If you purchase a subscription, payment information is processed by Apple through StoreKit. We do not have access to your payment details.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide Services: To process your fortune reading requests, generate personalized readings, and maintain your reading history.
  • Improve Our Services: To understand how you use the app and improve our features and performance.
  • Customer Support: To respond to your support requests and troubleshoot issues.
  • Security: To protect against fraud, unauthorized access, and other malicious activities.
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with applicable laws and regulations, including Apple App Store requirements and CCPA compliance.
  • Communications: To send you important updates about the app, security notifications, or account-related information.

3. How We Share Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We only share your data in the following limited circumstances:

  • Service Providers: We share data with third-party services necessary to operate the app:
    • Supabase (database and storage)
    • Firebase (analytics and crash reporting)
    • Google Gemini AI (image analysis and reading generation)
    • Apple (in-app purchases)
    • Adapty (subscription management)
  • Legal Requirements: If required by law, court order, or government authority.
  • Business Transfers: In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your data may be transferred as part of the business transaction.

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Storage Locations

  • Supabase servers (United States) - Your account data, readings, and images
  • Firebase servers (United States) - Analytics and crash reports
  • Google AI servers (United States) - Temporary image processing

4.2 Security Measures

  • All data transmission is encrypted using HTTPS/TLS protocols
  • Images and personal data stored in Supabase are protected by Row-Level Security (RLS) policies
  • Access to user data is restricted to authenticated requests
  • Passwords are hashed and never stored in plain text
  • Regular security audits and updates

5. Your Rights and Choices

5.1 California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) Rights

If you are a California resident, you have the following rights:

  • Right to Know: Request a copy of the personal information we have collected about you
  • Right to Delete: Request deletion of your personal information
  • Right to Opt-Out: Opt-out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell your information)
  • Right to Non-Discrimination: Exercise your privacy rights without discrimination

5.2 Account Management

  • Access Your Data: You can view your account information within the app settings
  • Delete Your Account: You can request account deletion by emailing us at umursoganci@gmail.com
  • Delete Specific Readings: You can delete individual readings from your reading history
  • Revoke Camera Access: You can revoke camera permissions in your iOS device settings
  • Opt-Out of Analytics: Analytics are only collected with your explicit consent during onboarding

5.3 Image Deletion

To request deletion of your uploaded images:

  1. Email us at umursoganci@gmail.com with your user ID
  2. Specify which images you want deleted (or request deletion of all images)
  3. We will process your request within 30 days

6. AI Processing Disclaimer

Important: Fortunia uses AI-powered technology (Google Gemini AI) to generate fortune readings. The readings are generated for entertainment purposes only and should not be considered as professional advice, predictions, or guarantees about your future.

The AI analyzes your photos and generates personalized readings based on cultural fortune-telling traditions. We do not claim that these readings provide accurate information about your life, health, relationships, or future.

7. Children's Privacy

Fortunia is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately at umursoganci@gmail.com, and we will delete that information.

8. International Users

If you are using Fortunia from outside the United States, please be aware that your information will be transferred to and stored in servers located in the United States. By using our App, you consent to this transfer.

9. Data Retention

  • Account Data: Retained until you delete your account
  • Reading History: Retained until you delete the specific reading or your account
  • Uploaded Images: Retained until you delete the associated reading or your account
  • Analytics Data: Retained for up to 26 months by Firebase Analytics
  • Crash Logs: Retained for up to 90 days in Firebase Crashlytics

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page. For significant changes, we may also notify you through the app or via email.

11. Apple App Store Compliance

This Privacy Policy complies with Apple App Store Review Guidelines Section 5.1 (Privacy and Data Use). We are transparent about our data collection practices and provide users with control over their personal information.

12. Contact Us

If you have questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

  • Email: umursoganci@gmail.com
  • Response Time: We aim to respond within 5-7 business days

For CCPA-related requests, please clearly state in your email that you are exercising your CCPA rights.
